## About S&P Global Ratewatch

**What is S&P Global Ratewatch?**
S&P Global Ratewatch is a leading provider of bank deposit rate data in the United States.
We collect rate and APY information from banks and credit unions nationwide and publish it
for market analysis, competitive benchmarking, and financial research — at no cost to your institution.

**Why are you calling?**
We are calling to collect your current deposit rates (CDs, Money Market, IRA, Savings, Checking).
This data helps your potential customers and financial analysts understand the competitive landscape.

**What do you do with the data?**
We publish aggregated deposit rate data to financial professionals, media, and consumers.
Your bank's rates help customers make informed decisions about where to keep their deposits.

**Is this free?**
Yes. Participation in our rate collection program is completely free for your institution.

## Products We Collect

**CDs (Certificates of Deposit)**
- Standard CDs at $10,000 deposit: 1, 3, 6, 12, 18, 24, 30, 36, 48, and 60 months
- Jumbo CDs at $100,000 deposit: same terms
- If your CD minimum is above $10,000, we collect only the Jumbo ($100,000) tier

**Money Market**
- Personal Money Market at balance tiers: $2,500 / $10,000 / $25,000 / $50,000 / $100,000 / $250,000
- Relationship/Premium Money Market (if applicable): same tiers plus relationship requirement details

**IRA Products**
- Liquid IRA (IRA Savings or IRA Money Market) at $10,000
- Fixed IRA CD (12-month term) at $10,000

**Savings**
- Regular savings account at $2,500

**Interest-Bearing Checking**
- No-relationship-requirement checking account at $2,500

**Specials and Promotions**
- Any current promotional or special deposit rates

## Data Definitions

**Rate vs. APY**
- **Rate** (or Interest Rate) is the stated annual interest rate before compounding
- **APY** (Annual Percentage Yield) reflects the effect of compounding over one year
- If your institution's rate and APY are the same, just say "they're the same" and Ryan will record both

**Balance Tiers**
For Money Market accounts, we collect rates at standard balance thresholds:
$2,500 / $10,000 / $25,000 / $50,000 / $100,000 / $250,000.
We skip any tier below your stated minimum deposit.

**Specials Fields**
For each special promotion Ryan will ask for:
1. Product type (CD, Money Market, Savings, etc.)
2. Term
3. Rate
4. APY
5. Minimum deposit
6. Maximum deposit (if any)
7. Qualification requirements (new money only, checking account required, etc.)
8. Whether the special applies to IRA accounts

## Privacy and Compliance

**Do Not Call**
If you wish to be removed from our call list, please say "do not call" or "remove my number."
Your request will be honoured immediately and the number will be added to our DNC registry.

**Callback Requests**
If you are unavailable, Ryan can schedule a callback at a time that works for you.
Callbacks are made Monday–Friday between 9 AM–1 PM and 3–5 PM Eastern time.

**Mergers and Acquisitions**
If your bank is in a merger or acquisition, please let Ryan know whether you will continue
as a separate division or undergo a full rebrand, and the expected timeline.
